Crystal Collector

Will the shiny crystals confuse you?!



The Rules:

Computer will choose a random number at the start of the game.

When the player is ready click on a crystal, each crystal have a random value assigned to it.

The player wins if their collected amount of crystals match the computers random number.

When the player wins or losses, the computer will change the value of each crystal.
